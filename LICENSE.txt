Data Sources and Attribution

This project incorporates publicly available environmental and demographic
datasets from United States government agencies.

1. U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)
   Dataset: EPA AirData
   Website:
   https://www.epa.gov/outdoor-air-quality-data

   Air quality monitoring station measurements and AQI-related pollutant
   summaries were derived from EPA AirData annual monitoring datasets.

2. United States Census Bureau
   Dataset: Population Estimates Program
   Website:
   https://www.census.gov/data/tables/time-series/demo/popest/
   2020s-total-cities-and-towns.html

   Population and demographic information were derived from Census Bureau
   place-level population estimate datasets and Gazetteer geographic files.

3. NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI)
   Dataset: Integrated Surface Database (ISD Lite)
   Website:
   https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/land-based-station/
   integrated-surface-database

   Wind vector and atmospheric movement visualizations were derived from
   NOAA ISD Lite historical weather station observations.

4. Esri
   Dataset/Service: ArcGIS World Imagery
   Website:
   https://www.esri.com/

   Satellite imagery tiles used for map rendering are provided by Esri.
